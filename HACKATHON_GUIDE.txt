PROJECT OVERVIEW
================
Khetibari: HarvestGuard - A mobile-first system that eliminates middlemen and 
connects farmers directly with consumers, ensuring farmers get 100% of their 
earnings with dynamic pricing based on real market demand.

KEY TALKING POINTS
==================

1. THE PROBLEM
   - Bangladesh loses 4.5 million metric tonnes of food grains annually
   - Farmers lose 20-30% to middlemen and distributors
   - Lack of direct market access
   - No real-time market information
   Solution: Direct connection, zero commission, real earnings

2. OUR SOLUTION
   - Direct Farmer-Consumer Marketplace
   - Dynamic Pricing (farmers earn MORE as demand increases)
   - Offline-First Architecture (works without internet)
   - Complete Crop Management System
   - Real-time Analytics Dashboard

3. CORE FEATURES

   A1: Landing Page with Problem Narrative
       - Visualize the $1.5 Billion problem
       - Show 34% annual crop loss
       - Clear solution pitch

   A2: Crop Batch Registration & Offline Storage
       - Register crop batches locally
       - Automatic batch ID generation
       - CSV export for record keeping
       - Form validation

   A3: Weather Service Integration
       - API ready for weather data
       - Risk prediction capability
       - Location-based forecasting

   A4: Batch Management Dashboard
       - View all registered batches
       - Track moisture levels
       - Monitor storage conditions
       - Batch history

   A5: Crop Health Scanner
       - Photo capture and analysis
       - Damage detection
       - Health scoring
